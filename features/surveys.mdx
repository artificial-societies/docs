Surveys let you ask questions to your society and quickly compare responses across personas.

## Building a Survey

<Steps>
    <Step title="Start a new Survey">

<Frame>    
    ![Create a Survey](/images/survey-selection.png)
</Frame>
<br />

    Click **create new test**, then choose **Survey**.
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Choose a question type">

<Frame>    
    ![Create a Survey](/images/question-types.png)
</Frame>
<br />

    There are three types of questions: **Single select**, **Multi select**, and **Open response**.
    Use the menu in the top right corner of the current question to switch between them.
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Write your question and add choices">

<Frame>    
    ![Create a Survey](/images/survey-example.png)
</Frame>
<br />

    For **single** and **multi** select questions, write your question and fill in the answer choices.
    Click **Add choice** to add options - up to 12 per question.
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Add more questions">

<Frame>    
    ![Create a Survey](/images/add-question.png)
</Frame>
<br />

    Click **Add question** to build a multi‑question survey.
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Launch your survey">

    When you’re finished, click **Ask** to launch the survey.
    Surveys with more questions take longer to run.
    </Step>
</Steps>

## Surveys FAQ

<Accordion title="How many choices can I add to a question?">
    You can add up to 12 choices for single and multi select questions.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I have fewer than two choices?">
    No. Single and multi select questions require at least two choices.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I reorder or delete choices?">
    Yes. Use the up and down arrows to reorder choices and the trash icon to delete them.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How many questions can I have in one survey?">
    You can have up to 20 questions per survey.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How long do surveys take to run?">
    Longer surveys take more time. Each additional question increases the time required to complete the survey.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I edit a survey after launching?">
    It is not currently possible to edit and relaunch surveys or duplicate them.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I export the results of my survey?">
    You can share the results of your survey using the 'share survey' link, but it is not currently possible to export full tabulated results.
</Accordion>
